The basics

  • Everyone starts at some central point, such as a subway station.
  • Victory Points are earned by getting to any POI(s).
  • Coins let the Runners move.
  • Challenges give Coins.
    • Challenges can only be started within 1/4 mile of a train station

Teams

  • The Runners start the game with 100 Coins. All other Coins must be earned with Challenges.
  • One Runner shares their location with a Chaser.
  • The Runners goal is to get to as many POIs as possible. Each POI = 1 Victory Point
    • When a POI is reached, it is removed from the map, and 2 more get placed
  • For each run, the Runners get a 15 minute head start. during this 15 minutes, Runners cannot select a challenge
    • Chasers have two options curing this time:
      • Stay where they are, and watch the Runners Location for the full 15 minutes
      • Freeze for 5 minutes, then move towards the center for 10 minutes, but NO LOCATION at all
  • The Chasers are just trying to tag any of the Runners
  • If any of the Runners get tagged, the run is over
  • When a tag happens, change teams, and the new Runners start from the current location

Misc

  • Chaser can split up, Runners cannot
    • Only one Chaser can use the location of the Runners, but they may relay any info
  • Coins are preserved, so your balance will remain from round to round.
  • On a tag, roles switch, and the new Runners get a 15 minute head start, but cannot do Challenges during this time.
  • Both teams start with 100 Coins, then have to use Challenges to earn more.
  • Only the Runners have to use Coins to travel
  • Both the Challenges and POI buttons are random, so if there is a dupe, just try again
